Digital Bill Book for Businesses

A modern, efficient solution for small and medium-sized enterprises to manage sales, expenses, and financial performance with ease.

Features

  • User Roles: Owner, Manager, and Employee for efficient team management.
  • Business Management: Create and manage businesses with intuitive UI.
  • Financial Records: Record daily sales and expenses with document attachments.
  • Reporting & Analytics: Generate reports with charts, profit/loss tracking, and export options.
  • Smart Features: OCR scanning, recurring expenses, and offline mode support.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: Flutter
  • Backend: Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions)
  • State Management: Flutter Bloc
  • Charting: FlChart
  • PDF Generation: Flutter PDF package

Future Enhancements

  • AI-based expense categorization
  • Profit forecasting and multilingual support
  • Integration with accounting software like QuickBooks

Development Timeline

Completion expected within 2-3 months of full-time development.
